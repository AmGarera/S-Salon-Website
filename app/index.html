SHEILA VILLADA


As the owner of Salon Etc., my goal is to have a team of talented individuals, providing the best customer service and making the salon feel like home. I am a nail tech and have been a licensed cosmetologist for 27 years. I deeply enjoy doing nails and pampering clients, taking my time with each and every client to give them a good manicure or pedicure so they do not feel rushed. I specialize in repairing ingrown toenails. Your nails are a way to speak your style without having to say a word!

Ashlea


I've been doing hair since 2004. I attend The Paul Mitchell School in Gastonia. I love doing hair. It makes me smile when my client leaves looking and feeling great about themselves. I do haircuts, color services, makeup, and special occasion hair. It's fun to come in and play with hair and makeup all day, like playing with dolls when I was a kid. Our clients are very important to us, and we get to know and love them on a personal level. It's great to be able to come to work and it not feel like it's work.

Bekah


Hi, my names Bekah! I'm originally from Mooresville but moved to the Gastonia area recently. I have two little potbelly pigs and a dog who are like my kids and also a fiancé who is like a 4th kid! (Lol!) On a serious note: growing up although I was a major tomboy I was the one doing other people's hair, whether it was for prom/special event or just to practice a new style. Shortly after graduating high school, I attended Aveda Institute of Charlotte for cosmetology. I have been in the industry for 3+ years now and I'm loving every second of it. I strive to constantly better myself in new techniques and I'm always open to learning. I believe that there's more to it than touching up new growth and trimming ends, it's about how I as a stylist make my clients feel leaving the salon. I will always make it my utmost priority to please my clients and to make them feel like a million dollars. "When you love what you do, you'll never work a day in your life.”
